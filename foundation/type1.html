Typography Hierarchy 1

Lato & Aleo

Hierarchy 1 uses a combination of serif and sans-serif typesfaces.

This hierarchy would be useful on pages where text content is heavier. The serif body typeface helps make reading larger bodies of text easier.

This hiearchy uses the major third scale with a base font size of 18px.
